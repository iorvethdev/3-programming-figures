John Carmack

Born on August 21, 1970, John Carmack is a computer scientist from the USA known of inventing the Games Comander Keen, Wolfenstein 3D, Doom, Quake, etc.

He is the Co-Founder of ID Software which published most of the games.

Carmack made the PC a gaming plataform starting with Commander Keen. Then he started making raycasters for the games catacomb 3D, hovertank 3D, and the most well known: Wolfenstein 3D!

After so much success he was going to make something bigger, something more like real 3D: DOOM! Which had elevators, stairs, better doors, and more.
