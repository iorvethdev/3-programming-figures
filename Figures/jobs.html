Steve Jobs

Born on February 24, 1955, Steve Jobs is a computer scientist from the USA known of inventing the iPhone.

He is the Co-Founded Apple Inc with Steve Wozniak on 1976 to develop ans sell Wozniak's Apple I computer, and they became famous a year later with production and sale of the Apple II, one of the 1st really successful mass-produced microcomputers.

After seeing the potential of the Xerox Alto in 1979, he made the unsuccessful Apple Lisa in 1983, followed by the success that was the macintosh from 1984, the first mass-produced GUI based computer.

The macintosh made the Desktop publishing industry with the adition of the apple laserWriter, the first laser printer with vector graphics and postscript.

Then he invented the iPhone, which ended the blackberry, and the other non touch screen with physical keyboard phones. He invented touch screen phones with a digital keyboard, before anyone did it, and also had the App Store for it which had apps.
