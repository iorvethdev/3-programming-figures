Inventor of the WWW and web browser

Tim Berners-Lee

Born 8 June 1955, Tim Berners-Lee is an English computer scientist known of inventing the World Wide Web, HTML, the URL system, and HTTP.

He is the Founder and the Director of the W3C.

Tim Berners-Lee implemented the first web browser and first web server.

He co-founded with her wife Rosemary Leith the World Wide Web Foundation. The World Wide Web Foundation is a nonprofit foundation which advocates a free and open web for everyone.
